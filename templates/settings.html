How to get your Supabase JWT (Auth Token)

The Auth Token is a short-lived JWT issued by Supabase when you log in to the TopRep app. You need to copy it from your browser session once and paste it below (or add it as a Vercel Environment Variable).

▶ Step-by-step: copy the token from the TopRep app
  1. Open the TopRep app in your browser and log in with your email and password.
  2. Open browser DevTools:
    • Chrome / Edge: F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I (Windows) / ⌘+Option+I (Mac)
    • Firefox: F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I (Windows) / ⌘+Option+I (Mac)
  3. Go to the Application tab (Chrome/Edge) or Storage tab (Firefox).
  4. In the left sidebar expand Local Storage and click the TopRep app origin (e.g. https://your-toprep-app.vercel.app).
  5. Look for a key that starts with sb- (e.g. sb-ahimfdfuuefesgbbnccr-auth-token). Click it to see the JSON value.
  6. In the JSON, find "access_token" and copy the long string value (it starts with eyJ).
  7. Paste it into the Auth Token (JWT) field below and click Save Settings.
  8. Click 🔌 Test Connection to confirm DealMaker can reach Supabase with your token.

💡 Vercel users: instead of saving the token here (it would be lost on redeploy), add TOPREP_AUTH_TOKEN=<your-token> in your Vercel project's Settings → Environment Variables panel, then redeploy. The token is automatically read from the environment — you do not need to save it in the Settings form.

🔒 Security: this token grants access to data as your Supabase user. Do not commit it to source control and do not share it. Tokens expire after ~1 hour; refresh by logging in to the TopRep app again and repeating the steps above.
